Horizon Orthopedics & Spine Institute Website

Designed and developed a professional healthcare website for Horizon Orthopedics, a specialized orthopedic and spine care center based in Nairobi, Kenya. Built using WordPress with WPBakery Page Builder, the site showcases key services including orthopedic surgery, spine treatments, physical therapy, and pediatric care. Emphasized clean design, fast performance, mobile responsiveness, and SEO optimization. Also integrated appointment request functionality and user-friendly navigation.
